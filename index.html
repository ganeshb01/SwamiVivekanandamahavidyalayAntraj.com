Bachelor of Computer Applications

A Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate degree focused on computer science, programming, and IT applications. It is ideal for students interested in software development, database management, and networking.

Bachelor of Arts

A Bachelor of Arts (BA) is an undergraduate degree awarded in the humanities, social sciences, liberal arts, and sometimes business or communication-related fields. It typically takes three to four years to complete, depending on the country and university.

Master of Arts

A Master of Arts (MA) is a postgraduate (graduate-level) degree awarded in humanities, social sciences, and arts-related disciplines. It typically takes one to two years to complete, depending on the country and university.
